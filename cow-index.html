Prepping a cow

This is the manual on how to cook beef. Beef is a very versatile flesh because it can be cooked to any degree of wellness, and different parts of the cow will have different textures and flavors. There are no wrong answers. The trick is familiarize yourself with the texture that you prefer. Chances are that others will like it too!

how to cook shank meat

Marinate the beef for several hours

Cook at 325 degrees Fahrenheit in a Dutch oven, until the fat begins to bubble

Add stock and simmer for 1 hour

Add chopped vegetables

Cover the Dutch oven and simmer for 1 1/4 hours at low-medium heat

how to fry a ribeye on a skillet

With butcher string, tie the ribeye around the sides such that it stands straight up.

Drizzle olive oil to a seasoned skillet under high heat. Lay the steaks on the skillet when oil begins to smoke

Add two cloves of garlic and a whole sprig of rosemary to the steak.

Add a hunk of butter. Take a spoon bast the melted butter on the steak

Remove ribeye when preferred wellness is reached. Let rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
